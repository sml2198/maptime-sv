What is OpenStreetMap? Why OSM?
OSM is like Wikipedia for maps. It's all open data!
Make an account: https://www.openstreetmap.org
Two ways to map: web iD Editor (easy) or JOSM (hard)
Use the iD editor to draw features in your browser: points, lines and areas
Try mapping somewhere that's not well mapped already. Remember to hit save, often.
HINTS: Square corners with "S", and use forward and back buttons.
Good practices: http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/ wiki/Good_practice
1. DO NOT COPY FROM GOOGLE . Don't even think about it.
2. Don't add things you're unsure about/things that aren't verifiable.
To learn how to add any kind of thing to the map: wiki.osm.org/wiki/Map_Features
3. Leave good changeset comments.
4. When in doubt, ask for help: www.learnosm.org wiki.osm.orghelp.osm.org
